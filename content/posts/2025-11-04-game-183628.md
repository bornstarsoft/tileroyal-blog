In an exciting turn of events for Nintendo fans, both Mario Kart World and Donkey Kong Bananza have been shortlisted for the prestigious 'Console Game of the Year' at the Golden Joystick Awards. This recognition highlights the impressive launch lineup for the Switch 2, showcasing how these titles have resonated with gamers and critics alike since their release.

The competition is fierce, with Donkey Kong Bananza also vying for 'Ultimate Game of the Year' alongside notable titles such as Hades II and Ghost of Ytei. As the gaming community gears up for the awards ceremony on November 21, 2025, its clear that Nintendo's latest offerings are making a significant impact in the industry.

With the Switch 2's innovative features and a growing library of games, the future looks bright for Nintendo. Will these beloved franchises continue to dominate the gaming landscape? Only time will tell, but one thing is for sure: the excitement surrounding these nominations is palpable among fans worldwide.
