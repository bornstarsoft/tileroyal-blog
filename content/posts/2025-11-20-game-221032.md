This playful interaction not only emphasizes the fun aspect of the game but also serves as a reminder of Woods' enduring popularity, even as he navigates his recovery from injuries that have kept him off the PGA Tour this season. As fans eagerly await his return to competitive golf, the PGA Tour 2K game offers a unique way to engage with Woods' legacy.
