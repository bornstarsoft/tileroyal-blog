Dogpile, the latest indie game from Studio Folly and Toot Games, offers a unique blend of Tetris-style puzzle mechanics and adorable dogs, creating an engaging experience for players. The game allows you to drag and drop various dog breeds onto a grid, combining them to create larger dogs, while introducing roguelite elements that enhance the gameplay. This combination of simplicity and strategic depth makes Dogpile a delightful addition to the puzzle genre.

While the core gameplay is enjoyable, its worth noting that some technical issues, such as crashes and audio glitches, can detract from the overall experience. However, the game also offers a relaxed mode for those who simply want to enjoy the joy of creating bigger dogs without the pressure of goals. As indie games continue to innovate, Dogpile stands out as a charming example of how to mix fun mechanics with a lighthearted theme.
