Get ready for a vibrant gaming experience as beXide announces the full release of *Fruit Mountain Party* on April 23rd! This anime-styled 3D puzzle game, which has garnered attention during its early access phase, is set to delight players on PC via Steam and Nintendo Switch, including the new Switch 2. The developers have taken player feedback to heart, focusing on enhancing gameplay stability and overall experience for this exciting launch.

In *Fruit Mountain Party*, players engage in a chaotic fruit battle, merging fruits to create larger ones while competing against friends or rivals online. With multiple game modes, including local play for up to four players, this game promises endless fun and laughter. The addition of features like improved frame rates on the Switch 2 and customizable plates adds to the excitement, making it a must-play for puzzle game enthusiasts.

As we approach the release date, anticipation builds for how *Fruit Mountain Party* will evolve the beloved *Fruit Mountain* franchise. Will you be joining the fruit battle?
