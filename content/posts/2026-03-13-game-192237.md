The upcoming pixel art block-pushing puzzle game, Hazard Pay, is set to launch this June for PS5, Switch, and PC, and it promises to deliver a unique blend of stealth, survival horror, and narrative choice. Players will step into the role of a government cleanup specialist tasked with navigating a sinister research facility filled with horrifying bio-organisms and uncovering dark secrets hidden within its walls. The games handcrafted puzzle levels and clean-up mechanics, such as erasing blood trails and destroying evidence, add an intriguing layer of gameplay that sets it apart from traditional puzzle games.

As players traverse the eerie environment of the Institut fr Proteintherapie, they will face moral dilemmas that affect the narrative's outcome, making each playthrough a unique experience. With the ability to decide the fate of trapped scientists and the looming threat of deadly creatures, Hazard Pay invites players to question their role within the system. Will you be just another expendable worker, or will you rise to expose the truth?

With its compelling narrative and innovative gameplay mechanics, Hazard Pay is shaping up to be a must-play title for puzzle enthusiasts and horror fans alike. Are you ready to uncover the chilling secrets of the facility?
